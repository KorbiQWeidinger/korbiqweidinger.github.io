## About

I have a huge passion for software development, especially the craft of making software easier to understand, change, and trust. That bias started early: I worked at [CQSE](https://www.cqse.eu/), a company that exists for the sole purpose of software quality, static analysis, and helping teams see what their code is really doing.

That background made me very good at spotting rough edges. It also taught me the harder lesson: great engineering is not only about raising the bar, it is about knowing when the bar is high enough to ship. I am still learning that balance between moving fast and keeping the codebase something future humans can live with.

Away from the keyboard, I am usually somewhere between "training sensibly" and "signing up for another thing." I climb 🧗, paddle 🛶, swim 🏊, do triathlons 🏊‍♂️🚴‍♂️🏃‍♂️, lift weights 🏋️, and have tried enough sports that my calendar sometimes looks like a confused fitness app.
